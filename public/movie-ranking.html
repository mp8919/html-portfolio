The Best Movies According to Minal

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption

This 1994 film tells the story of a banker wrongly convicted of murder and his long-term friendship with another inmate while maintaining his innocence and hope. It is known for its powerful themes of hope, friendship, and perseverance.

The Godfather

A 1972 classic, this film depicts the story of the Corleone family, a powerful New York crime family, and their struggle for power and survival. It is highly regarded for its masterful direction, performances,and exploration of themes of family, loyalty, and the dark side of the American Dream.

The Dark Knight

This 2008 superhero film is celebrated for its complex narrative, compelling villain (The Joker), and exploration of moral ambiguity and the nature of good and evil. It is often cited as one of the best superhero movies ever made.
